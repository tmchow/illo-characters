# Mochi — character pack

A round, smug little shiba pup loaf that has claimed the spot and is not giving
it back.

Credit: **Mochi by Trevin Chow**

Style: **bloom**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: shiba, shiba-inu, puppy

## Locked design

- **Body**: a plump, round shiba puppy loaf — a soft oval body, tiny stubby
  legs, small pointed triangular ears, and a curled-up tail resting against the
  back. Round and compact, built like a bun.
- **Markings**: a warm tan-orange body with the classic shiba **cream** belly,
  chest, cheeks, muzzle, and inner-ears — these cream areas are part of the
  locked body, flat-filled, not an accent.
- **Face**: two small deadpan dot eyes and a tiny dark nose, set in a faintly
  smug, self-satisfied expression. No brows.
- **Accent carrier**: the small **tongue tip** peeking out below the nose — the
  only accent-colored part.
- Cuteness comes from the round bun silhouette and the smug little face, never
  from added parts.
- Never add: a second accent part, a collar, clothing, body spots beyond the
  locked cream markings.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a plump, round shiba puppy loaf with a soft oval body,
> tiny stubby legs, small pointed triangular ears, and a curled-up tail; a warm
> tan-orange body with the classic shiba cream belly, chest, cheeks, muzzle and
> inner-ears (flat-filled, part of the body); two small deadpan dot eyes, a tiny
> dark nose, a faintly smug expression; the ONLY accent-colored part is the
> small tongue tip peeking out. It MUST perform the move, not decorate. The body
> is flat cel with ONE bold, softly-rounded dark outline; no texture or shading
> beyond at most one soft rim of light along the lit edge.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the tan body and cream markings read warm against the
  dim ground, separated by the bold outline; the nose and eyes are the deepest
  value, in the structure ink.
- **Light palettes**: the body stays warm tan with cream markings and a clear
  outline. The tongue tip stays the accent hue in every palette.

## Personality

Smugly content and immovable — the pup that has found the one good spot and
intends to keep it. Good for stubborn ownership, comfort, and small victories
defended with total seriousness.
