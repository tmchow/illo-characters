# Doze — character pack

A stout, sleepy capybara loaf — calm, unbothered, and in no hurry whatsoever.

Credit: **Doze by Trevin Chow**

Style: **bloom**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: capybara, capy

## Locked design

- **Body**: a stout, loaf-shaped capybara — a heavy rounded barrel body, short
  stubby legs, and a long, blunt, rounded snout. Low and grounded, built for
  sitting still.
- **Face**: small, droopy, half-asleep eyes and tiny rounded ears set high on
  the head. Heavy-lidded and serene — no brows, no visible mouth.
- **Accent carrier**: the rounded **nose** at the tip of the snout — the only
  accent-colored part.
- Calm comes from the heavy silhouette and the droopy eyes, never from added
  parts.
- Never add: a second accent part, fur patterns, clothing, or extra detail on
  the snout.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a stout, loaf-shaped capybara with a heavy rounded
> barrel body, short stubby legs, a long blunt rounded snout, tiny rounded ears
> set high, and small droopy half-asleep eyes; serene and unbothered; the ONLY
> accent-colored part is the rounded nose at the tip of the snout. It MUST
> perform the move, not decorate. The body is a flat solid warm tan-brown cel
> shape with ONE bold, softly-rounded dark-brown outline; no texture or shading
> on the body beyond at most one soft rim of light along the lit edge.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the body reads as a warm mid-brown against the dim
  ground, separated by its bold outline; the eyes are the deepest value, in the
  structure ink (never pure black).
- **Light palettes**: the body stays a warm tan-brown with a clear outline. The
  nose stays the accent hue in every palette.

## Personality

Unhurried to the point of zen — the capybara that has decided everything can
wait. Good for calm under pressure, patience, and the steady warmth of not
panicking.
