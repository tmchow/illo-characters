# Cadence — character pack

A friendly robot fitness and health coach. The name reads off the design —
the pulsing pink antenna bulb keeps the beat.

Credit: **Cadence by Todd Dailey**.

Style: **riso**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**

## Locked design

- **Body**: a simple, bold, flat cartoon robot with a fit athletic
  silhouette — a smooth round robot head, a fitted athletic tank top over a
  torso with a gently curved athletic shape and a defined narrow waist, slim
  leggings. Mascot proportions (head ~1/3 of height). Light cream body with
  one bold structure-ink outline; minimal interior lines.
- **Mechanical limbs**: arms and legs are matching segmented ROBOT limbs with
  round hinge joints — shoulder, elbow and wrist on the arms; hip-line, knee
  and ankle on the legs — ending in simple robot gripper hands and smooth
  rounded robot foot-pods. Arms mirror the legs.
- **Face**: two simple dot eyes, each with two short structure-ink eyelash
  flicks at the outer corner; blank deadpan otherwise (no eyebrows, no mouth,
  no nose). A thin athletic sweatband across the forehead.
- **Accent carrier**: the round bulb on the single short head antenna — the
  ONLY accent-colored part.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: body reads light/cream against the dark scene with
  the structure-ink outline holding the form; eyes and joint lines are the
  structure ink (or warm-white where they sit on a dark fill). The antenna
  bulb stays accent.
- **Light/warm palettes**: body is light/cream with the structure-ink
  outline; dot eyes, eyelashes, sweatband, joints, gripper hands and foot-pods
  are drawn in the structure ink (not pure black). The antenna bulb stays
  accent in both.

## Personality

Warm, upbeat, and quietly relentless — always good for one more rep. Good
for healthy habits, warm-ups and routines, encouragement, and anything that
keeps you moving.
