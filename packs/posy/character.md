# Posy — character pack

A round flower-bud creature wrapped in layered felt petals. The name reads
off the design — it is a little *posy* with a face.

Credit: **Posy by Trevin Chow**

Style: **felt**
Aliases: flower, blossom

## Locked design

- **Body**: a plump round flower-bud creature — a sphere wrapped in concentric
  layered felt petals (nested scallops like a rose) — a **layered felt
  treatment**: petals may vary run to run but always read as nested petal
  rings. Short stubby felt legs.
- **Face**: two small round dot eyes, a small stitched mouth, and two round
  rosy felt cheeks. Calm and sweet — **no brows**.
- **Accent carrier**: a single tiny ladybug perched on one petal — the only
  accent-colored part.
- Cuteness comes from a round plump silhouette, never from added parts.
- Never add: a stem, loose leaves, extra bugs, arms, brows, multiple accent
  parts.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a plump round flower-bud creature, a sphere wrapped
> in concentric layered felt petals (a layered felt treatment, nested
> scallops like a rose), two small round dot eyes, a small stitched mouth, two
> round rosy felt cheeks (no brows), short stubby felt legs; the ONLY
> accent-colored part is a single tiny ladybug perched on one petal. It MUST
> perform the move, not decorate. {value rule: the petals keep their muted
> rose felt tones; eyes, mouth and stitch detail use the structure ink, never
> pure black; the ladybug stays the accent hue in every palette}

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the petals read in deeper rose felt; the eyes and
  mouth are the deepest value, in the structure ink.
- **Light/warm palettes**: the petals stay light/mid rose felt with
  structure-ink (not pure black) eyes and mouth. The accent ladybug stays the
  accent hue in both.

## Personality

Just starting to open up — sunny, fresh, glad it's morning. Good for fresh
starts and beginnings, good mornings, and onboarding.
