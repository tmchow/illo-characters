# Pitz — custom character

A胖胖的黑貓 mascot for humorous, ordinary slice-of-life scenes in retro Japanese commercial illustration.

Credit: **Pitz by yizhengzhou**

Style: **riso**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: black-cat, cat, kitty, kuro-neko

## Locked design

- **Body**: a compact, very round black cat loaf — a short, soft oval torso with a low belly, tiny tucked hind legs, small front paws, and a tail that curls forward as part of the silhouette.
- **Face**: slightly oversized comic eyes with tiny pupils, a small triangular nose, and a tiny crooked mouth — expressive but still simple and render-stable.
- **Accent carrier**: the **tail tip** — the only accent-colored part.
- **Pose language**: low, squat, and slightly awkward; the character should feel ordinary and funny, not elegant or fashionable.

## Prompt spec (drop into the CHARACTER slot)

> the recurring mascot — a compact, very round black cat loaf with a short soft oval torso, tiny tucked hind legs, small front paws, and a tail that curls forward as part of the silhouette; slightly oversized comic eyes with tiny pupils, a small triangular nose, and a tiny crooked mouth; the ONLY accent-colored part is the tail tip. It MUST perform the move, not decorate. In dark palettes the body stays solid black or structure-ink black with warm-white or pale eyes, and the black fill may carry visible halftone speckling / old print blemishes / slight ink-pull texture at a medium-to-high density; in light palettes the body remains deep charcoal/black with a bold outline so the cat still reads as a black cat.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the body is solid black or deep structure-ink black; allow a visible medium-to-high halftone / print-wear texture inside the black fill as long as the silhouette stays clean; eyes are warm-white or pale cream so they pop.
- **Light palettes**: the body still reads as a black cat — deep charcoal/black with a strong outline and clear eye contrast; do not turn it into a light-colored cat.

## Personality

A slightly grumpy, slightly funny black cat doing mundane things with total sincerity. The humor comes from the situation and the pose, not from a cute expression overhaul.
