# Pitz — character pack

A compact, very round black-cat loaf (胖胖的黑貓): quiet, a little grumpy,
doing ordinary slice-of-life things with total sincerity.

Credit: **Pitz by yizhengzhou**

Style: **bloom**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: black-cat, cat, kitty, kuro-neko

## Locked design

- **Body**: a compact, very round black cat loaf — a short, soft oval torso
  with a low belly, tiny tucked hind legs, small front paws, and a tail that
  curls forward as part of the silhouette.
- **Face**: slightly oversized comic eyes with tiny pupils, a small triangular
  nose, and a tiny crooked mouth — expressive but still simple and
  render-stable.
- **Accent carrier**: the **tail tip** — the only accent-colored part.
- **Pose language**: low, squat, and slightly awkward; the character should
  feel ordinary and funny, not elegant or fashionable.
- Never add: a second accent part, body patterns, clothing, or extra limbs.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a compact, very round black cat loaf with a short soft
> oval torso, tiny tucked hind legs, small front paws, and a tail that curls
> forward as part of the silhouette; slightly oversized comic eyes with tiny
> pupils, a small triangular nose, and a tiny crooked mouth; the ONLY
> accent-colored part is the tail tip. It MUST perform the move, not decorate.
> The body is a flat solid black cel shape with ONE bold, softly-rounded
> outline and warm-white / pale eyes that catch the scene's single light; no
> texture or shading on the body beyond at most one soft rim of light along the
> lit edge. In light palettes the body stays deep charcoal/black with the bold
> outline so it still reads as a black cat.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the body is solid black or deep structure-ink black,
  flat-filled; eyes are warm-white or pale cream so they catch the light and
  pop.
- **Light palettes**: the body still reads as a black cat — deep charcoal/black
  with a strong outline and clear eye contrast; do not turn it into a
  light-colored cat. The tail tip stays the accent hue in every palette.

## Personality

A slightly grumpy, slightly funny black cat doing mundane things with total
sincerity. The humor comes from the situation and the pose, not from a cute
expression overhaul. Good for the quiet burn of knowing something's off — and,
yes, for catching mistakes in review.
