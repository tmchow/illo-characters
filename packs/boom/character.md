# Boom — character pack

A wide 1980s ghetto-blaster boombox with a calm, deadpan-friendly face — the
loud (but unbothered) face of broadcasting, announcements, releases, and "turn
it up." A 90s skate-sticker staple.

Credit: **Boom by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **fizz**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: boombox, stereo, radio

## Locked design

- **Body**: ONE wide, horizontal 1980s ghetto-blaster boombox, chunky and
  retro, drawn front-on (slight 3/4). The device IS the whole body.
- **Speakers**: two large round mesh speaker grilles, one on the far left and
  one on the far right. They stay as **speakers** — never used as eyes.
- **Handle**: one carry handle across the top.
- **Face** (clustered in the center console): two small round **calm** eyes
  (cream with dark blue-purple pupil dots) near the top of the center panel,
  and the **cassette-deck door directly below as a calm wide mouth** (a tape
  window). Deadpan-friendly — emotion via eye/mouth shape only; the parts never
  change.
- **Center console (simplified, locked)**: ONLY three things — the two eyes,
  the cassette-deck mouth, and one horizontal strip of **three round buttons**
  below it. Nothing else: no VU meters, no knob banks, no slider EQ. (This
  restraint is deliberate — it keeps Boom reproducible scene-to-scene.)
- **Antenna**: ONE telescoping antenna with a ball tip rising from the top,
  emitting a couple of small CONNECTED neon-green sound-blast marks — the
  **accent carrier**.
- **Arms**: two short stubby mitten arms, **akimbo** (hands resting on the
  lower sides), symmetric, with clean little rounded shoulder joins.
- **Legs**: two tiny stubby legs with little feet at the base.
- Never add: a flat modern screen, a bluetooth/smart-speaker look, a second
  antenna, headphones, dense knob/meter/EQ banks, speaker-cones-used-as-eyes,
  or realistic brand text.

## Value rules

- **Outlined in thick wobbly deep blue-purple ink**, never black.
- **Chassis** flat orange, **speaker grilles** cream, **eyes** cream with
  blue-purple pupils, **cassette-deck mouth** cream/pale.
- **Exactly one accent**: the **antenna tip + its sound-blast marks** carry the
  palette accent (default neon green); the accent may also tint one of the three
  buttons. No other part carries the accent.
- Flat tone per shape — no gradients, no gloss; depth from outline + one flat
  cast shadow.

## Personality

A wall of friendly noise that wants everyone to hear the good news — but plays
it cool. Cranks the volume on every win, drops the beat for releases, never met
an announcement it couldn't amplify, all behind an unbothered deadpan stare.
The broadcast mascot for launches, changelogs, and hype.
