# Sprout — character pack

A small seed that has just sprouted: a plump seed body with one single leaf
on a thin stem growing from its head. The name reads off the design.

Credit: **Sprout by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **riso**

## Locked design

- **Body**: one plump rounded seed — a soft fat oval, slightly wider at the
  bottom. No roots, no soil, no flower.
- **Face**: two simple dot eyes, completely blank and deadpan — no eyebrows,
  no mouth, ever.
- **Leaf**: ONE short thin stem from the top of the head ending in a SINGLE
  small simple leaf — the **accent carrier** and the only accent-colored
  part. Never a second leaf.
- Small stubby arms and stubby legs.
- Cuteness comes from proportion and roundness, never from added parts.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a plump rounded seed body (a soft fat oval), two
> simple dark dot eyes, blank deadpan (no eyebrows, no mouth), ONE short thin
> stem on top of its head ending in a SINGLE small leaf, small stubby arms
> and legs; the head-leaf is the ONLY accent-colored part. It MUST perform
> the move, not decorate. The body is light paper-colored with the bold
> structure-ink outline.

## Value rules

- **All palettes**: the body stays light/paper-colored with the structure-ink
  outline; the eyes are structure ink; only the head-leaf takes the accent.

## Personality

A patient gardener of small things — tends, waters, waits, deadpan. The
character for growth, compounding, and anything that gets bigger one small
effort at a time.
