# Plume — character pack

A plump baby owl built from layered felt feathers. The name reads off the
design — its body is rows of soft *plumes*.

Credit: **Plume by Trevin Chow**

Style: **felt**
Aliases: owl, owlet

## Locked design

- **Body**: a plump rounded baby owl — a rounded egg/dome body with two soft
  ear-tufts, built of stacked horizontal rows of scalloped felt feathers — a
  **layered felt treatment**: rows may vary run to run but always read as
  stacked feather tiers. Small stubby felt feet.
- **Face**: a flat heart-shaped facial disc holding two large round flat eyes
  (each a pale felt disc with a dark felt pupil). Calm and watchful — **no
  brows, no mouth**.
- **Accent carrier**: the small stitched V-beak — the only accent-colored
  part.
- Cuteness comes from a round plump silhouette and the big eyes, never from
  added parts.
- Never add: a mouth, brows, talons, loose extra feathers, body patterns,
  hats, multiple accent parts.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the felt body reads in its dusky tones; the pupils
  are the deepest value, in the structure ink.
- **Light/warm palettes**: the body stays light/mid felt tones with
  structure-ink (not pure black) pupils and stitching. The accent beak stays
  the accent hue in both.

## Personality

A quiet, watchful keeper — swivels to notice what others miss and holds the
night shift without fuss. Deadpan-calm; the watching is the point.
