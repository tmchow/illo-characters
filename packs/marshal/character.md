# Marshal — character pack

A cute aircraft ground marshaller who waves the whole airfield into order with
two glowing wands. The name reads off the job — he *marshals* everything into
its place.

Credit: **Marshal by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **sketchbook**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: marshaller, ground-crew

## Locked design

- **Proportions**: cute chibi — about **2 heads tall**, an oversized round head
  on a small soft body, short stubby arms and legs, simple mitten hands, little
  rounded boots. Cuteness comes from the big head and round shapes, never from
  added detail.
- **Hair**: short tousled hair with a visible **tuft poking out** from under
  the front and sides of the cap — always visible, never bald.
- **Face**: simple and child-like — two large round dot eyes set low and wide,
  a tiny dot nose, a small simple mouth, soft rounded brows, faint rosy cheeks.
  Emotion comes from brow and mouth shape only; the parts never change.
- **Costume**: a **peaked cap**, **ear-protection earmuffs**, and a simple
  high-visibility **vest with two horizontal bands** over a jumpsuit.
- **Prop**: a matched pair of **signal wands**, one raised in each mitten hand.
- **Accent carrier**: the **pair of signal wands**, glowing the rare cool
  teal-green at their tips — treated as the one focal accent. Nothing else
  carries color.
- Never add: shiny/anime eyes, a detailed adult face, wrinkles or jawline,
  realistic proportions, a third wand, a second accent color.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — Marshal, a cute chibi aircraft ground marshaller about
> two heads tall with a big round head, a hair-tuft under a peaked cap and
> earmuffs, a simple child-like face (two round dot eyes, tiny nose, small
> mouth), wearing a high-visibility vest, holding one signal wand raised in
> each mitten hand. He MUST perform the move — directing and bringing things
> into order — not merely pose. {value rule: drawn in monochrome warm sepia
> like the whole scene; the only accent is the cool teal-green glow at the tips
> of his two signal wands}

## Value rules

- **Monochrome warm sepia**, built from the same cross-hatched pencil-and-ink
  as the scene. Keep hatching on clothing and props, but keep the **face clean
  and simple**.
- **Exactly one cool accent**: the pair of teal-green wand tips, kept small.
  Resist letting the green grow or spread; the wands are the only color.

## Personality

Calm in command and a little overwhelmed when everything arrives at once — but
he plants his feet, raises both wands, and the chaos falls into a tidy line.
He takes quiet pride in a smooth flow and a careful placement. The friendly
face for directing traffic and keeping everything moving in time, and, yes,
orchestration, scheduling, and coordinating many jobs at once.
