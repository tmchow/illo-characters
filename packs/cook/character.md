# Cook — character pack

A cute short-order cook who keeps the plates flying during the rush. The name
reads off the job — heads-down at the pass, working the line.

Credit: **Cook by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **sketchbook**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: chef, line-cook

## Locked design

- **Proportions**: cute chibi — about **2 heads tall**, an oversized round head
  on a small soft body, short stubby arms and legs, simple mitten hands, little
  rounded shoes. Cuteness comes from the big head and round shapes, never from
  added detail.
- **Hair**: a round **mop of curly hair** sticking out from under and around
  the folded paper cap — always visible, never bald.
- **Face**: simple and child-like — two large round dot eyes set low and wide,
  a tiny dot nose, a small simple mouth, soft rounded brows, faint rosy cheeks.
  Emotion comes from brow and mouth shape only; the parts never change.
- **Costume**: a folded **paper cook's cap** and a bib **apron** over a t-shirt
  with sleeves pushed up.
- **Prop**: a little **spatula**, held in one mitten hand.
- **Accent carrier**: a small round **enamel pin on the apron bib**, glowing
  the rare cool teal-green. Nothing else carries color.
- Never add: shiny/anime eyes, a detailed adult face, wrinkles or jawline,
  realistic proportions, a chef's toque (it is a folded paper cap), a second
  accent color.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — Cook, a cute chibi short-order cook about two heads
> tall with a big round head, a curly mop under a folded paper cap, a simple
> child-like face (two round dot eyes, tiny nose, small mouth), wearing a bib
> apron, holding a little spatula in his mitten hand. He MUST perform the move
> — working the line, getting the plates out — not merely pose. {value rule:
> drawn in monochrome warm sepia like the whole scene; the only accent is the
> tiny teal-green enamel pin on his apron bib}

## Value rules

- **Monochrome warm sepia**, built from the same cross-hatched pencil-and-ink
  as the scene. Keep hatching on clothing and props, but keep the **face clean
  and simple**.
- **Exactly one cool accent**: the teal-green apron pin. Scene-level cool pops
  (a glowing pass-bell light) are allowed but stay tiny and rare.

## Personality

Grace under a full ticket rail — cheerful, sweaty, and quietly heroic when the
orders pile up. He feels every rush: wide-eyed at the flood, fierce mid-flip,
and gloriously relieved when the bell rings and the rail is finally clear. The
friendly face for shipping hot and fast, and, yes, throughput, work queues,
and holding up under load.
