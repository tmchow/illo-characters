# Pop — character pack

A cracked-open soda-pop can with a goofy face, erupting with fizz — the loud,
celebratory face of releases, launches, hype, and "ship it." Pure
psychedelic-beverage energy.

Credit: **Pop by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **fizz**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: soda, cola

## Locked design

- **Body**: ONE upright soda-pop can, drawn 3/4 view (the can IS the whole
  body, never a bottle, never a glass). Standing on tiny stubby legs.
- **Label**: a flat color label band wrapping the can with ONE bold graphic
  mark on it (a chunky starburst or lightning shape), no realistic brand text.
- **Top**: the can is cracked OPEN — a popped pull-tab lid with a fizz crown
  erupting from the opening. Always open, never sealed.
- **Face** (on the label, center of the can): two large round cartoon eyes,
  cream with dark blue-purple pupils, and one wide open happy grin below.
  High-energy and expressive — emotion via eye/grin shape only.
- **Fizz crown**: the signature **accent carrier** — the spray of fizz and
  bubbles erupting from the open top, drawn CONNECTED to the can rim (one
  shape, so it survives a cutout). Carries the accent color.
- **Arms**: two short stubby mitten arms from the sides of the can.
- **Legs**: two tiny stubby legs with little feet at the base.
- Never add: a bottle/glass, a sealed lid, a drinking straw, realistic brand
  logos, condensation as the main feature, or a detached/floating fizz cloud.

> the recurring mascot — Pop, a cracked-open soda-pop can with a goofy face:
> an upright can standing on tiny stubby legs, a flat label band with one bold

> and one wide grin on the label, two short stubby mitten arms, and its
> signature fizz crown of bubbles erupting from the popped pull-tab top
> (connected to the rim). It MUST perform the move, not pose. {value rule:
> thick wobbly blue-purple outlines, flat loud fills; the accent color lives on
> the erupting fizz crown and 1-2 bubbles}

## Value rules

- **Outlined in thick wobbly deep blue-purple ink**, never black.
- **Label band** one bold flat color (default cobalt blue), **can metal**
  cream, **eyes** cream with blue-purple pupils.
- **Exactly one accent**: the **fizz crown** carries the palette accent
  (default neon green); accent may tint 1–2 stray bubbles. No other part
  carries the accent.
- Flat tone per shape — no gradients, no gloss; depth from outline + one flat
  cast shadow.
- **Cutout note**: the fizz crown stays because it is drawn connected to the
  rim; loose detached bubbles are scene-only.

## Personality

A bottled-up burst of enthusiasm that finally got shaken and opened — can't
contain itself, sprays joy everywhere, toasts every win. The celebratory
hype-mascot for launches, releases, and announcements.
