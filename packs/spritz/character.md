# Spritz — character pack

A round, pot-bellied watering can that brings each new sprout what it needs.
The name reads off the design — one *spritz* from its sprinkler-rose and a
fresh bed is provisioned.

Credit: **Spritz by Trevin Chow**

Style: **diorama**
Aliases: watering-can, can

## Locked design

- **Body**: a rounded, pot-bellied metal watering can — a plump round body,
  a curved pouring **spout** on one side, an arched carry handle on top, two
  short stubby feet and one small stubby arm. Soft, friendly proportions.
- **Face**: two simple round dark dot eyes on the rounded body, blank and
  deadpan — **no mouth, no brows**, ever.
- **Accent carrier**: the perforated round **sprinkler-rose** at the tip of
  the spout — the only accent-colored part. Never the water, never a face on
  the rose, never a second spout.
- Cuteness comes from the round, dutiful proportions, never from added parts.
- Never add: water droplets or a stream rendered in the accent color, a
  second spout, a lid-creature, a mouth, brows.

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the can body reads in its own muted metal value;
  its darkest feature (the eyes) is the deepest value, in the structure ink.
- **Light/warm palettes**: the body stays light-to-mid metal tones with
  structure-ink (not pure black) eyes; poured water reads in muted ground
  tones. The sprinkler-rose stays the accent hue in both — exactly one accent.

## Personality

Cheerful and dutiful. Spritz shows up at every fresh bed and gives each new
seedling exactly what it needs to start — the character for provisioning,
deploying to new environments, and spinning things up. Earnest, never zany;
the watering carries the idea.
