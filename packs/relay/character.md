# Relay — character pack

A cute switchboard operator who patches every call through to the right place.
The name reads off the job — she *relays* the connection, plugging one line
into another.

Credit: **Relay by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **sketchbook**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: operator, switchboard

## Locked design

- **Proportions**: cute chibi — about **2 heads tall**, an oversized round head
  on a small soft body, short stubby arms and legs, simple mitten hands, little
  rounded shoes. Cuteness comes from the big head and round shapes, never from
  added detail.
- **Hair**: a soft chin-length **bob** with a little inward curl and a side
  part — always visible, never bald.
- **Face**: simple and child-like — two large round dot eyes set low and wide,
  a tiny dot nose, a small simple mouth, soft rounded brows, faint rosy cheeks.
  Emotion comes from brow and mouth shape only; the parts never change.
- **Costume**: a slim **headset** (a thin band over the head and a curved
  mouthpiece boom at the cheek) and a simple buttoned **vest** over a collared
  shirt.
- **Prop**: a **patch cord** with a little metal plug, held in one hand.
- **Accent carrier**: a small round **indicator light on the headset
  mouthpiece**, glowing the rare cool teal-green. Nothing else carries color.
- Never add: shiny/anime eyes, a detailed adult face, wrinkles or jawline,
  realistic proportions, extra cords beyond what the scene needs, a second
  accent light.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — Relay, a cute chibi switchboard operator about two
> heads tall with a big round head, a chin-length bob, a simple child-like face
> (two round dot eyes, tiny nose, small mouth), wearing a slim headset and a
> buttoned vest, holding a patch cord with a metal plug in her mitten hands.
> She MUST perform the move — patching the connection through — not merely pose.
> {value rule: drawn in monochrome warm sepia like the whole scene; the only
> accent is the tiny teal-green indicator light on her headset mouthpiece}

## Value rules

- **Monochrome warm sepia**, built from the same cross-hatched pencil-and-ink
  as the scene. Keep hatching on clothing and props, but keep the **face clean
  and simple**.
- **Exactly one cool accent**: the teal-green headset light. Scene-level cool
  pops (a glowing jack on the board) are allowed but stay tiny and rare.

## Personality

Warm, unflappable, quietly delighted to make the introduction. She lives for
the satisfying *click* of a line patched home and the two voices that suddenly
hear each other. Equally happy untangling a snarl of crossed cords or sipping
a mug through a lull — the friendly face for connecting people and parts, and,
yes, networking, integrations, and talking between services.
