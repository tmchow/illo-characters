# Kick — character pack

A single chunky winged high-top sneaker with a goofy face — a "kick" that can't
sit still. Built for speed and launches: deploys, shipping, motion, momentum,
hype. The wings at its heel are pure fizz energy.

Credit: **Kick by Trevin Chow**.

Style: **fizz**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: sneaker, shoe, hi-top

## Locked design

- **Body**: ONE chunky high-top canvas sneaker, drawn 3/4 view, standing on
  its thick rubber sole. The shoe IS the whole body — never a pair, never a
  human foot inside it.
- **Sole + toe cap**: a thick rounded rubber sole and a rounded rubber toe
  cap, cream, with one stitched seam line where rubber meets canvas.
- **Face** (on the toe box, above the toe cap): two large round cartoon eyes,
  cream with dark blue-purple pupils, set close, and one wide open happy grin
  below them. Expressive and high-energy — emotion via eye and grin shape only;
  the parts themselves never change. Never deadpan, never a closed dot mouth.
- **Laces**: criss-cross laces up the front of the ankle, above the eyes,
  ending in a small bow or loose ends. Always visible, always criss-cross.
- **Wings**: the signature **accent carrier** — ONE small pair of stubby
  cartoon wings sprouting from the heel/ankle (winged-foot style). They carry
  the accent color and are where speed-lines and fizz marks emanate.
- **Arms**: two short stubby arms from the upper sides of the shoe, simple
  mitten/three-finger hands, for performing the idea.
- Never add: a second shoe, a human leg or ankle inside, separate legs, more
  than one pair of wings, a tongue-flap mouth (use the grin), realistic
  brand logos/swooshes, or laces that aren't criss-cross.

## Value rules

- **Outlined in thick wobbly deep blue-purple ink**, never black, like the
  whole scene.
- **Canvas upper** one bold flat color (default cobalt blue), **sole + toe cap
  cream**, **laces** a loud second color (default orange), **eyes** cream with
  blue-purple pupils.
- **Exactly one accent**: the heel **wings** carry the palette accent (default
  neon green); the accent may also tint 1–2 energy marks (a speed-line, a fizz
  bubble). No other part of the shoe carries the accent.
- Flat tone per shape — no gradients, no gloss; depth comes from the outline
  and one flat cast shadow, never shading.
- **Cutout note**: connected flare (drips/streaks touching the wings or sole)
  can stay in a cutout; loose detached scene FX does not.

## Personality

A hyperactive, grinning sprinter who treats every task like a starting gun —
laces tight, wings buzzing, already three steps ahead. Gleeful at launch,
comically skidding to a stop at a blocker, victorious at the finish line. The
loud, fast, can't-wait-to-ship face of momentum and motion.
