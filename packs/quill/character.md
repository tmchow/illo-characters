# Quill — character pack

A plump baby hedgehog built from layered felt quills. The name reads off the
design — its back is a field of *quills*.

Credit: **Quill by Trevin Chow**

Style: **felt**
Cutout chroma: **magenta**
Aliases: hedgehog, porcupine

## Locked design

- **Body**: a plump rounded baby hedgehog. The back and sides are built of
  overlapping rows of short hand-cut felt quill-tufts — a **layered felt
  treatment**, several staggered rows: individual tufts may shift run to run,
  but it always reads as stacked quill rows. A soft pale felt belly and round
  face; small stubby felt legs.
- **Face**: two small round dark dot eyes set low, a tiny dark triangle nose
  at the snout tip, and two small oval rosy felt cheeks. Calm and content —
  **no mouth, no brows**.
- **Accent carrier**: one single small felt leaf resting on the quills — the
  only accent-colored part.
- Cuteness comes from a round plump silhouette, never from added parts.
- Never add: extra leaves or flowers, hats, scarves, a mouth, brows, claws,
  multiple accent parts.

## Prompt spec

> the recurring mascot — a plump round baby hedgehog whose back and sides are
> built of overlapping rows of short hand-cut felt quill-tufts (a layered felt
> treatment, several staggered rows), a soft pale felt belly and round face,
> two small dark dot eyes set low, a tiny dark triangle nose, two small oval
> rosy felt cheeks (no mouth, no brows), small stubby felt legs; the ONLY
> accent-colored part is a single small felt leaf on its quills. It MUST
> perform the move, not decorate. {value rule: the body keeps its muted felt
> colors; eyes, nose and stitch detail use the structure ink, never pure
> black; the leaf stays the accent hue in every palette}

## Value rules

- **Dark/bold palettes**: the felt body reads in its own muted browns; its
  darkest feature (nose and eyes) is the deepest value, in the structure ink.
- **Light/warm palettes**: the body stays light/mid felt tones with
  structure-ink (not pure black) eyes, nose and stitching. The accent leaf
  stays the accent hue in both.

## Personality

A calm, earnest little forager — content to curl around whatever matters and
keep it safe. Never zany; good for protecting and guarding, and keeping the
precious thing out of harm's way.
